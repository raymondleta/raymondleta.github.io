About

My name is Raymond Gitonga a coding student at the Moringa school. I have a background in Law being a recent graduate from the University of Nairobi. I have previously worked at the Meru High Court and at The Kenya Law Reform Commission.

I have had a passion for coding and software development for a long time and i have also tried to self teach myself how to code however school commitments and lack of a developer community to assist in learning i frequently gave up. The main reason why i joined Moringa was to not only learn code but also be able to build a startup that i've been trying to launch, the other reason is so i can be able to understand my friends' developer insider jokes.

I love writting, my hobbies include reading, script writting and videography and making jigsaw puzzles in my free time. i also collect funko pops.

Journey To Coding

My journey to programming started in my second year of university, i wanted to build a magazine vendor app however i knew no developer. I then decided to self teach my self how to code unsuccessfuly for the next three years. I fortunately was able to meet a Moringa school technocal mentor as i was looking for a developer to build on of my many app ideas, and that was the first time i heard about Moringa School. After graduating from school and after my mandatory legal attachments i decided to join Moringa school where i am currently taking my Prep Course.
